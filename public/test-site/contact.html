Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote for my project?

You can request a quote by filling out our TakeShape form above, calling us directly, or sending us an email. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail and provide you with a comprehensive quote.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations for all projects. During this consultation, we'll discuss your needs, preferences, and budget to help determine the scope of your project.

How long will my project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. During our consultation, we'll provide you with an estimated timeline for your specific project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. We can provide proof of insurance upon request.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. The specific terms of the warranty depend on the type of project and will be outlined in your contract.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer financing options through our partner financial institutions. We can discuss these options during your consultation.
